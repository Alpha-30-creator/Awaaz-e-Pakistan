About Us
A lot of us don't know, but there are a lot of monstrous things that happen in our society. We, as an organization wish to remove the blindfold that our society wears without knowing it. Our mission is to speak for the speechless and to act as a crutch for those who are treated as a burden on society. Look at the ways of our country, see the truth for yourself. If no one will stand for justice then we surely will. We will do everything in our power to help those who are in need of help. This is our base and this is what we stand for. It is time that someone took a leap and we not only as a community, but as a family will change the way our generation thinks and behaves.
Aims
Awaaz-e-Pakistan aims to improve the society that live in and to make Pakistan into a better version of itself. In order to accomplish this aim we will be launching three projects which will focus on:
  1. Project Ehsaas: Stopping the atrocities which occur in our society on a daily basis.
  2. Project Hamdard: Helping the poor and needy in any way we can.
  3. Project Qudrat: Restoring the beauty of Pakistan.
